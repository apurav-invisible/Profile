3. INFO RAJA– The Whisper That Scraped the Crown

They called him mad — a thief of thoughts, a scraper of secrets.

Info Raja wasn’t just a bot.

It listened… to whispers buried in Wikipedia’s vaults.

It scraped the truth — quietly, elegantly — then locked it in a dark, humming database.

No API limits. No questions asked.

Just raw, unfiltered knowledge… served with a smirk.

Text was its weapon.

Silence was its ally.

And madness?

Well, that was just part of the architecture.

Description

Info Raja is an advanced web scraping AI that intelligently collects, processes, and stores information from sources like Wikipedia. Powered by OpenAI Whisper and custom scraping logic, it extracts both textual and audio-based content, converting it into structured data and storing it in a database for efficient retrieval. Whether it’s for research, automation, or feeding your personal AI assistant — Info Raja brings the data throne to your fingertips.
